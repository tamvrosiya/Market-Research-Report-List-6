<p><ul><li>PET-CT</li><li>SPECT-CT</li><li>PET-MR</li><li>OCT</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical multimodal imaging market comprises various imaging technologies that enhance diagnostic capabilities. PET-CT combines positron emission tomography with computed tomography for precise tumor localization. SPECT-CT merges single-photon emission computed tomography with CT for improved functional and anatomical imaging. PET-MR integrates positron emission tomography with magnetic resonance imaging, offering superior soft tissue contrast. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) provides high-resolution imaging, primarily for ocular applications. Additionally, "Other" encompasses emerging technologies and hybrid systems that facilitate advanced diagnostic imaging solutions.</p></p>
