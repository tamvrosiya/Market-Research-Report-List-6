<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) films market is segmented into three main types: Polyester TPU, Polyether TPU, and Polycaprolactone TPU. Polyester TPU offers excellent abrasion resistance and mechanical strength, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether TPU is valued for its flexibility and hydrolysis resistance, commonly used in medical and food packaging. Polycaprolactone TPU exhibits superior elasticity and low-temperature performance, used in specialized applications like adhesive films and soft touch coatings, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
