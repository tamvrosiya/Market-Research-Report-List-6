<p><p>The Ionic Air Purifier market comprises various technologies, with photocatalytic oxidation and bi-polar ionization being prominent types. Photocatalytic oxidation harnesses light to activate a catalyst that breaks down pollutants into harmless substances. In contrast, bi-polar ionization generates charged ions that attach to airborne particles, neutralizing them and promoting their aggregation for easier removal. Both methods enhance indoor air quality by reducing pathogens and pollutants, appealing to health-conscious consumers and contributing to a healthier living environment.</p></p>
