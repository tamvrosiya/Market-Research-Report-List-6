<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) System</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive keyless entry access systems market primarily comprises two types: Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) and Remote Keyless Entry (RKE). PKE systems allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically using a key or remote, relying on sensors to detect the presence of the key fob. In contrast, RKE systems require the user to press a button on a remote control to unlock the doors or start the engine. Both systems enhance convenience and security for vehicle users.</p></p>
