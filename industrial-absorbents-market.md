<p><p>The industrial absorbents market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Natural Inorganic Industrial Absorbents:** These absorbents, such as clay and diatomaceous earth, are sourced from natural mineral deposits, offering excellent performance in liquid absorption due to their porous structure and high surface area.</p><p>2. **Natural Organic Industrial Absorbents:** Derived from organic materials like peat and sawdust, these absorbents are biodegradable and effective in absorbing oils and chemicals, making them eco-friendly options.</p><p>3. **Synthetic Industrial Absorbents:** Engineered from polymers, these absorbents provide enhanced performance and versatility, often designed for specific applications, such as chemical spills or hazardous liquid cleanup.</p></p>
