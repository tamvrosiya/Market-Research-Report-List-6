<p><p>Cloud Radio Access Network (C-RAN) is a network architecture designed to enhance the capacity and efficiency of mobile communication systems. Enhanced mobile broadband and the need for efficient spectrum usage are propelling this market forward. Moreover, the rise of IoT and smart city initiatives further accelerates the need for advanced networking solutions like C-RAN.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the adoption of Open RAN architectures, which promote interoperability among different vendors, and the growing focus on artificial intelligence (AI) within network management to optimize performance and predictive maintenance.
