<p><p>The glucose monitoring devices market is primarily categorized into two types: Self-Monitoring Devices and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) Devices. Self-Monitoring Devices, such as fingerstick glucometers, enable individuals to check their blood glucose levels at specific intervals. In contrast, CGM devices provide real-time glucose readings continuously throughout the day, using sensors placed under the skin. Both types are essential for managing diabetes, offering varying levels of convenience and data accuracy to help users maintain optimal blood sugar levels.</p></p>
