<p><p>Utility Customer Information Systems (CIS) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CIS solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and lower upfront costs, ideal for utilities seeking flexibility and reduced IT burdens. In contrast, on-premises CIS systems are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing enhanced control over data and security, but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both approaches cater to different operational needs and strategic priorities.</p></p>
