<p><p>The MEMS inkjet printhead market is divided into two primary types: IDM (Integrated Device Manufacturers) and foundry. IDM companies design, manufacture, and sell their inkjet printheads, maintaining control over the entire production process, which enhances customization and innovation. In contrast, the foundry market involves third-party manufacturers that provide fabrication services to IDM companies or other clients, allowing them to focus on design and application without investing in production infrastructure. Both approaches drive advancements in printhead technology and applications.</p></p>
