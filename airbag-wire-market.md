<p><p>The airbag wire market is divided into two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket. OEM airbag wires are produced by the vehicle manufacturers or their approved suppliers, ensuring compatibility and adherence to safety regulations. In contrast, aftermarket airbag wires are made by third-party companies and can cater to repairs or replacements in vehicles not covered by OEM. Both segments serve distinct consumer needs, with OEM focusing on reliability and safety, while aftermarket emphasizes cost-effectiveness and availability.</p></p>
