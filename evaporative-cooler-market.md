<p><p>An evaporative cooler, also known as a swamp cooler, is a cost-effective and energy-efficient cooling device that uses the natural process of evaporation to lower air temperature. It functions by blowing warm air through water-saturated pads, resulting in cooled, humidified air being circulated throughout a space. This technology is particularly beneficial in dry climates where humidity levels are low.</p><p>The evaporative cooler market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of energy efficiency, rising temperatures, and the need for sustainable cooling solutions.
