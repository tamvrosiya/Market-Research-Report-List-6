<p><ul><li>Coronary Catheterization</li><li>Echocardiogram</li><li>Intravascular Ultrasound</li><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Computed Tomography</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac imaging market encompasses various diagnostic modalities essential for evaluating heart health. Coronary catheterization visualizes coronary arteries, aiding in identifying blockages. Echocardiograms utilize ultrasound waves to assess heart structure and function. Intravascular ultrasound provides detailed imaging of blood vessels through a catheter. Positron emission tomography (PET) reveals metabolic activity, useful for detecting ischemia. Computed tomography (CT) offers cross-sectional imaging to evaluate coronary artery disease, while magnetic resonance angiography (MRA) visualizes blood vessels using magnetic fields and radio waves, providing detailed anatomical information.</p></p>
