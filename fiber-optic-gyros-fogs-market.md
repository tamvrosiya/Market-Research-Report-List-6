<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Gyros (FOGs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric FOG</li><li>Resonant FOG</li><li>Stimulated Brillouin Scattering FOG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optic Gyros (FOGs) are precision sensors that utilize light transmitted through fiber optics to measure rotation. The market is segmented into several types: </p><p>Interferometric FOGs leverage interference patterns of light to achieve high accuracy, making them suitable for aerospace and navigation. Resonant FOGs use the resonant frequency of light waves for lower-cost applications with moderate precision. Stimulated Brillouin Scattering FOGs exploit the interaction of light with acoustic waves for enhanced sensitivity and stability, primarily targeting advanced industrial and military applications.</p></p>
