<p><p>The Protein Detection and Quantitation Market is categorized into two main types: Instrument Detection and Reagent Detection. Instrument Detection involves the use of advanced tools such as spectrophotometers, chromatographs, and mass spectrometers that directly measure protein levels by analyzing samples. Reagent Detection, on the other hand, relies on chemical reagents and kits that facilitate protein identification or quantification through various biochemical assays. Both methods play essential roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and biopharmaceutical development, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
