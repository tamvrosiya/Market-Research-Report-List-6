<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging market comprises three primary types: glass, paper & paperboard, and plastics. Glass offers excellent protection against contamination and is impervious to gases, making it ideal for injectable drugs. Paper and paperboard are lightweight, eco-friendly options suitable for secondary packaging and labeling, providing sufficient protection while being cost-effective. Plastics are versatile and widely used due to their durability, lightweight nature, and barrier properties, making them suitable for various forms of medications, including tablets and liquids.</p></p>
