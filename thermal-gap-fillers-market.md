<p><ul><li>Sheet Gap Filling Material</li><li>Liquid Gap Filling Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal gap fillers market encompasses two primary types: sheet gap filling materials and liquid gap filling materials. Sheet gap fillers are pre-formed materials designed to be placed between heat-generating components and heat sinks, providing effective thermal management in a compact form. Liquid gap fillers, on the other hand, are applied in a liquid state and cure to form a solid bond, adapting to complex geometries for improved thermal conductivity. Both types enhance thermal performance in electronic applications.</p></p>
