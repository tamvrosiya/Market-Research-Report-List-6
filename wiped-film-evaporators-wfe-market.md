<p><p>Wiped Film Evaporators (WFE) are efficient heat exchangers used for separating solvents from viscous materials. The two primary market types are vertical and horizontal wiped film evaporators. Vertical WFE designs feature a vertical orientation, promoting effective gravity drainage and are often favored for large-scale outputs. In contrast, horizontal WFE designs enhance processing flexibility and are suitable for specific applications requiring precise thermal control. Both types share common applications in chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries for concentration and purification processes.</p></p>
