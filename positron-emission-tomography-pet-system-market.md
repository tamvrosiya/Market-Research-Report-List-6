<p><ul><li>Direct Imaging</li><li>Indirect Imaging</li><li>Alternative Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) system market encompasses three main imaging types. </p><p>Direct imaging involves capturing gamma rays emitted from radiotracers in real-time, providing high-resolution images of metabolic processes. Indirect imaging uses scintillation detectors to convert gamma rays into visible light, which is then digitized for analysis, offering a cost-effective alternative. Alternative imaging techniques explore integrations with technologies like MRI or CT, enhancing diagnostic capabilities and patient comfort by combining functional and anatomical information. Each method serves distinct clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
