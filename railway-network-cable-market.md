<p><p>The railway network cable market comprises two primary types: signal cables and communication cables. Signal cables are essential for transmitting data related to train movements, ensuring safety and operational efficiency by facilitating the communication between trackside equipment and control centers. Communication cables, on the other hand, are used for voice and data transmission among railway personnel and systems, supporting tasks such as monitoring, control, and real-time updates. Together, these cables enhance the overall functioning and safety of railway networks.</p></p>
