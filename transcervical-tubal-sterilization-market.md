<p><p>The Transcervical Tubal Sterilization market comprises two main types: Tubal Ligation and Transcervical Implants. Tubal Ligation involves surgically blocking or sealing the fallopian tubes to prevent pregnancy, a traditional method offering permanent contraception. In contrast, Transcervical Implants, like the Essure system, involve inserting small coils into the fallopian tubes through the cervix, promoting tissue growth to create a barrier against sperm. Both methods provide women with effective and long-term options for fertility control, catering to diverse patient preferences and needs.</p></p>
