<p><ul><li>Patient Cooling Systems</li><li>Patient Warming Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Noninvasive Temperature Management System market comprises Patient Cooling Systems and Patient Warming Systems. Patient Cooling Systems are designed to reduce body temperature in cases of fever, heatstroke, or post-surgery, enhancing patient recovery while minimizing complications. Conversely, Patient Warming Systems are utilized to elevate body temperature in scenarios like hypothermia or during surgeries to prevent intraoperative hypothermia. Both systems are critical in maintaining optimal thermal balance in patients, thereby improving clinical outcomes and ensuring safer healthcare delivery.</p></p>
