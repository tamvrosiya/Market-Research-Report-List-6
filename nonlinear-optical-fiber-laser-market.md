<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonlinear optical fiber laser market encompasses two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a consistent and uninterrupted beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring steady output, such as material processing and telecommunications. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers deliver light in short bursts or pulses, enabling high peak power for applications like cutting, welding, and medical treatments. Both types leverage nonlinear optical effects to enhance performance and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
