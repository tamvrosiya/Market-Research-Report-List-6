<p><ul><li>Double Flange bearing</li><li>Split bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic clip bearings market primarily features two types: double flange bearings and split bearings. Double flange bearings are designed with flanges on both sides, providing stability and preventing lateral movement, making them ideal for applications requiring robust support. In contrast, split bearings consist of two halves that can be easily assembled around a shaft, facilitating installation and maintenance. Both types offer advantages such as reduced friction, corrosion resistance, and lightweight properties, catering to various industries like automotive, manufacturing, and consumer goods.</p></p>
