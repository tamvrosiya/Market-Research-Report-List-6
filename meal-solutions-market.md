<p><p>The Meal Solutions market includes three primary types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE meals are fully prepared and require no further cooking, providing convenience for immediate consumption. RTH meals require minimal heating, typically in a microwave or oven, allowing for quick preparation. RTC meals come with raw ingredients and recipes, requiring cooking by the consumer, offering a balance of convenience and the experience of home-cooked meals.</p></p>
