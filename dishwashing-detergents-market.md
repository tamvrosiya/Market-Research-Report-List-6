<p><strong>The Dishwashing Detergents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saponification</li><li>Non-saponification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dishwashing detergents market is categorized into two main types: saponification and non-saponification. Saponification detergents are derived from natural fats and oils, creating soap-like compounds that effectively clean dishes while being biodegradable. In contrast, non-saponification detergents are synthetic formulations, often utilizing surfactants and enzymes to remove grease and stains without the soap properties. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and environmental considerations, influencing their usage in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
