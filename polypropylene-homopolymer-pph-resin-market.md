<p><strong>The Polypropylene Homopolymer (PPH) Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotactic Polypropylene</li><li>Atactic Polypropylene</li><li>Syndiotactic Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polypropylene Homopolymer (PPH) resin market is primarily segmented into three types: isotactic polypropylene, atactic polypropylene, and syndiotactic polypropylene. Isotactic polypropylene, characterized by its high crystallinity and strength, is widely used in rigid applications. Atactic polypropylene, with its amorphous structure, offers flexibility and is often utilized in adhesives and coatings. Syndiotactic polypropylene combines features of both, providing a balance of strength and transparency, making it suitable for various packaging and automotive applications.</p></p>
