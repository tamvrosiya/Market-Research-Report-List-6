<p><ul><li>Brake Booster</li><li>Tandem Master Cylinders (TMCs)</li><li>Brake Fluid Reservoir</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake actuation systems market includes various components essential for vehicle braking performance. Brake boosters enhance brake force by using vacuum or hydraulic pressure, making it easier for drivers to engage the brakes. Tandem master cylinders (TMCs) are critical for distributing hydraulic pressure to different brake circuits, ensuring effective braking in all situations. Brake fluid reservoirs store the hydraulic fluid necessary for the brake system to operate, maintaining optimal fluid levels for reliable braking functionality and safety.</p></p>
