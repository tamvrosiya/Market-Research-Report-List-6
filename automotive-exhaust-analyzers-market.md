<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dispersive Infrared</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyser</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive exhaust analyzers are devices used to measure and analyze vehicle emissions. **Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR)** analyzers detect gases like CO and CO2 by measuring infrared absorption. **Flame Ionization Detectors (FID)** measure hydrocarbons by ionizing them in a hydrogen flame. **Chemiluminescence Analyzers** detect NOx emissions by reacting them with ozone, producing light proportional to concentration. **Other** market types include electrochemical sensors and portable analyzers, offering versatility for various emission testing needs. These technologies ensure compliance with environmental regulations and improve vehicle performance.</p></p>
