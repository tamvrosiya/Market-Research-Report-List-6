<p><p>The Neck Orthosis System market comprises two primary types: Inlay Type and Externally Assisted devices. Inlay Type orthoses are designed to be integrated within existing neck support structures, providing additional stabilization and comfort without altering the user’s routine. Externally Assisted orthoses, on the other hand, are standalone devices that offer external support and alignment, often used for patients with severe neck issues. Both types aim to alleviate pain, support recovery, and enhance the overall quality of life for individuals with neck-related disorders.</p></p>
