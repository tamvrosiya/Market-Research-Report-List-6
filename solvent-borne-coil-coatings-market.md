<p><p>The solvent-borne coil coatings market encompasses three main types: primer coatings, back coatings, and top coatings. Primer coatings enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance, serving as a base layer for metal substrates. Back coatings protect the reverse side of metal sheets, preventing moisture and corrosion. Top coatings provide a durable finish, offering aesthetic appeal and weather resistance. Together, these coatings contribute to the longevity and performance of metal products used in various applications, including construction and appliances.</p></p>
