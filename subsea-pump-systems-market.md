<p><p>The subsea pump systems market comprises various types, including Helical Axial Flow and Twin Screw pumping systems. Helical Axial Flow pumps are designed for high flow rates at lower pressures, making them suitable for applications like boosting fluids from the seabed to surface facilities. In contrast, Twin Screw pumping systems utilize two intermeshing screws to provide efficient and continuous transfer of liquids, capable of handling varying viscosities. Both types are critical for enhancing oil and gas production in challenging subsea environments.</p></p>
