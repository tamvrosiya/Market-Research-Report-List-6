<p><p>The medical grade sodium alginate market is categorized into three viscosity types: low viscosity (below 150 mPa.s), medium viscosity (150 to 400 mPa.s), and high viscosity (above 400 mPa.s). Low viscosity alginate is primarily used in applications requiring rapid gelling and ease of handling, while medium viscosity offers a balance of gel strength and fluidity, making it suitable for wound dressings. High viscosity alginate provides superior gel formation and stability for more demanding medical applications, such as controlled drug delivery and advanced wound healing.</p></p>
