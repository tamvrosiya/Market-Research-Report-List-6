<p><ul><li>Interdigitated Structure</li><li>Concentric Structure</li><li>Aperiodic Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D structure lithium-ion batteries market encompasses three primary designs: interdigitated, concentric, and aperiodic. Interdigitated structures feature alternating electrode layers, enhancing ion transport and energy density. Concentric structures involve layered electrodes arranged in a cylindrical format, optimizing space and energy efficiency. Aperiodic structures, characterized by irregular patterns, aim to improve performance by maximizing surface area and minimizing resistance. Each design offers unique benefits in energy storage applications, promoting advancements in electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
