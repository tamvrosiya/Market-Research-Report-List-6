<p><p>The medical ventilators market is categorized into two main types: single-use and reusable ventilators. Single-use ventilators are designed for one-time use, ensuring sterile, disposable options that minimize infection risk, particularly beneficial in emergency settings. These devices typically offer advanced functionalities and are favored in long-term care facilities, providing cost-effectiveness and adaptability for various patient needs.</p></p>
