<p><p>D&B liability insurance encompasses various market types, primarily Directors and Officers (D&O) Insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) Insurance. D&O Insurance protects corporate leaders from personal losses due to legal proceedings arising from their decisions. Conversely, E&O Insurance safeguards professionals from claims of negligence or inadequate work that may harm clients. In the context of Design and Build projects, both insurance types help mitigate financial risks associated with management decisions and professional services, ensuring comprehensive coverage for stakeholders.</p></p>
