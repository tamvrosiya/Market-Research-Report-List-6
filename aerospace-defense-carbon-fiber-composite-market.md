<p><p>The Aerospace & Defense Carbon Fiber Composite Market is categorized into two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic composites. Thermosetting types are characterized by a rigid structure that is formed when heated, offering high strength and stability, commonly used in aircraft components. In contrast, thermoplastic types can be reshaped upon heating, providing flexibility and easier manufacturing processes, making them suitable for more versatile applications. Both types contribute significantly to weight reduction and enhanced performance in aerospace and defense industries.</p></p>
