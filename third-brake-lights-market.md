<p><p>Third brake lights, also known as center high-mounted stop lamps (CHMSL), are additional brake lights mounted at the center of a vehicle's rear, typically above the trunk or rear window. These lights have become a standard feature in modern vehicles due to their effectiveness in reducing rear-end collisions.</p><p>The Third Brake Lights Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing vehicle production, rising safety regulations, and growing consumer awareness about road safety.
