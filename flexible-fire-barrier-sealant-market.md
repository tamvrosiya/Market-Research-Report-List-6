<p><strong>The Flexible Fire Barrier Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Fire Barrier Sealant market encompasses two primary types: elastomeric and intumescent. Elastomeric sealants are flexible, durable, and designed to protect against smoke and fire by maintaining their integrity under extreme conditions. In contrast, intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective char that insulates and prevents the spread of flames. Both types are essential for ensuring fire safety in construction, providing critical barriers in walls, floors, and joints to enhance fire resistance and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
