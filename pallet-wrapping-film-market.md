<p><p>The pallet wrapping film market consists of several types, including cast film, blown film, pre-stretch film, and others. Cast film is known for its clarity and flexibility, making it ideal for tight wrapping. Blown film offers superior strength and puncture resistance, suitable for heavy loads. Pre-stretch film is specially engineered to be stretched before application, reducing material use and cost. Other types may include specialty films for specific applications, such as UV-resistant or biodegradable options, catering to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
