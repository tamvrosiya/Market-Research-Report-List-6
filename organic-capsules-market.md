<p><p>The organic capsules market is primarily divided into two types: hard capsules and soft capsules. Hard capsules are typically composed of two pieces that are filled with powdered ingredients; they offer stability and longer shelf life, making them ideal for dry formulations. Soft capsules, on the other hand, consist of a single, gelatin-like shell that encapsulates liquid or oil-based substances, providing enhanced bioavailability. Both types cater to the growing demand for organic supplements and medications, emphasizing natural ingredients and consumer health preferences.</p></p>
