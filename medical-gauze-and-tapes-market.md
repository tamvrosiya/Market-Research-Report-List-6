<p><p>The medical gauze and tapes market is segmented into several types. Woven gauze is made from interlaced fibers, providing breathability and absorbency, ideal for wound care. Non-woven gauze offers superior fluid absorption and reduces fiber shedding, making it suitable for sensitive skin. Fabric tapes are durable and stretchable, ensuring secure adherence, while paper tapes are gentle on the skin, facilitating easy removal. Plastic tapes provide water resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for various medical applications and different wound types.</p></p>
