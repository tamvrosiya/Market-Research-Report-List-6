<p><strong>The Digital PCR Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR</li><li>Droplet Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital PCR Platform market comprises two main types: Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR and Droplet Digital PCR. Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR utilizes integrated microfluidic technologies to perform multiple reactions in parallel on a single chip, enhancing sensitivity and precision in nucleic acid quantification. In contrast, Droplet Digital PCR partitions a PCR reaction into thousands of droplets, allowing for precise quantification of target DNA by analyzing each droplet separately, making it suitable for low-abundance targets and rare mutation detection.</p></p>
