<p><p>The endocrine testing market encompasses various technologies for diagnosing hormonal disorders. Tandem mass spectrometry is a highly sensitive method used to analyze complex biological samples, enabling precise measurement of multiple hormones simultaneously. Immunoassays, on the other hand, utilize antigen-antibody interactions to detect hormone levels, offering rapid results and ease of use. Both technologies play crucial roles in clinical laboratories, facilitating accurate hormone profiling for conditions such as diabetes, thyroid disorders, and adrenal insufficiencies, thus enhancing patient care and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
