<p><ul><li>Water-Borne Coatings</li><li>Solvent Borne Coatings</li><li>Powder Coatings</li><li>UV Cured Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The on-site maintenance coatings market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Water-Borne Coatings**: These are environmentally friendly, low-VOC options that use water as a solvent, making them easy to clean and quick-drying. </p><p>2. **Solvent-Borne Coatings**: Comprising organic solvents, these offer excellent durability and adhesion, suitable for harsh environments.</p><p>3. **Powder Coatings**: Applied as a dry powder, these coatings provide a tough finish and are often used for metal surfaces due to their resistance to chipping.</p><p>4. **UV Cured Coatings**: Cured using ultraviolet light, these coatings offer rapid drying and superior durability, ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
