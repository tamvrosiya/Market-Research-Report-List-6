<p><strong>The Vehicle Seatbelt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle seatbelt market can be categorized into two main types: passive and active seatbelts. Passive seatbelts automatically secure occupants upon door closure, requiring no action from passengers, enhancing safety with minimal effort. In contrast, active seatbelts require manual engagement from the user, although they often incorporate features like pre-tensioners that tighten upon collision. Both types contribute significantly to automotive safety, with passive systems emphasizing user-friendliness and active systems focusing on user control and responsiveness in emergency situations.</p></p>
