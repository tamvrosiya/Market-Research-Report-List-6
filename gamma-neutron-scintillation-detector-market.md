<p><p>The Gamma Neutron Scintillation Detector market comprises three main types: solid, liquid, and gas material detectors. Solid material detectors use scintillation crystals to convert gamma and neutron radiation into visible light for detection, offering high resolution and stability. Liquid material detectors utilize scintillation liquids, providing enhanced sensitivity and the ability to detect lower energy emissions. Gas material detectors rely on gas-filled chambers, suitable for detecting high-energy gamma rays and neutrons. Each type serves specific applications across various industries such as healthcare, nuclear, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
