<p><strong>The Inventory Tank Gauging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Level Instruments</li><li>Continuous Level Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inventory Tank Gauging System market is primarily segmented into two types: point level instruments and continuous level instruments. Point level instruments are designed to indicate specific levels, alerting operators when tanks reach predetermined heights. In contrast, continuous level instruments provide real-time measurements of liquid levels within tanks, allowing for ongoing monitoring and management. These systems are crucial for optimizing inventory management, preventing overflow, and ensuring safety in storage operations across various industries, including oil, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
