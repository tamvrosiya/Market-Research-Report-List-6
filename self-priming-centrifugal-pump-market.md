<p><p>The self-priming centrifugal pump market is categorized into two main types: multistage self-priming pumps and single-stage self-priming pumps. Multistage self-priming pumps are designed to handle higher pressures and increased flow rates by employing multiple impellers, making them suitable for applications requiring elevated lift. In contrast, single-stage self-priming pumps are simpler and more compact, ideal for low to moderate pressure applications. Both types are widely used in various industries, including agriculture, water treatment, and construction, due to their efficiency and ease of operation.</p></p>
