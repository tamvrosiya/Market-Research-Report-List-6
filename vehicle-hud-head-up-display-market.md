<p><p>The Vehicle Head-up Display (HUD) market comprises three main types: W-HUD, AR-HUD, and C-HUD. W-HUD (Windshield HUD) projects essential information directly onto the windshield, enhancing driver focus on the road. AR-HUD (Augmented Reality HUD) overlays digital information on the real-world view, providing advanced navigation and safety features. C-HUD (Combiner HUD) uses a small transparent screen to display information, allowing drivers to receive updates without diverting attention from driving. Each type enhances driver awareness and safety.</p></p>
