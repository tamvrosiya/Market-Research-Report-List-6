<p><p>The disposable nitrile examination gloves market is primarily classified into two types: powdered and powder-free gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a medical-grade powder, typically corn starch, to facilitate easier donning and removal. However, they may pose risks of allergic reactions or contamination. In contrast, powder-free gloves are produced without powder, eliminating these risks and making them ideal for sensitive applications, such as surgery or handling chemicals. The choice between the two largely depends on user preference and specific use cases.</p></p>
