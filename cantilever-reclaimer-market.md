<p><p>Cantilever reclaimers are crucial equipment in material handling, especially in sectors like mining and bulk storage. The market can be categorized into two main types: wheel reclaimers and chain reclaimers. Wheel reclaimers use rotating wheels to effectively gather and transfer materials, offering high mobility and efficiency. In contrast, chain reclaimers utilize a chain mechanism for material retrieval, providing robust durability for heavy-duty applications. Both types serve distinct operational needs, optimizing productivity in stockyard management and material processing.</p></p>
