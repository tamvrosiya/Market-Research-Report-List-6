<p><strong>The Commercial Baggage Handling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID Commercial Baggage Handling System</li><li>Barcode Commercial Baggage Handling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Baggage Handling System market is categorized primarily into two types: RFID and Barcode systems. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) systems utilize radio waves to automatically identify and track baggage, enhancing speed and accuracy in handling. In contrast, Barcode systems rely on printed barcodes scanned at various points, offering a cost-effective solution but requiring direct line-of-sight for scanning. Both technologies aim to streamline baggage processing in airports, reduce loss, and improve operational efficiency in the aviation industry.</p></p>
