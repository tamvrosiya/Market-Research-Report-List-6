<p><p>The Silicone Structural Glazing market includes various types distinguished by their framing and design features. **Four-sided structural** glazing uses silicone on all sides for full support, while **two-sided structural** relies on silicone bonding on two edges. **Slope** glazing is designed for inclined surfaces, allowing effective water drainage. **Stepped glass** incorporates staggered edges for aesthetic appeal, and **U-shaped** systems offer unique framing options. **Total vision systems** prioritize unobstructed views, while **others** encompass unconventional designs. Additionally, the **aluminum structural framing** market provides durable and lightweight support for these glazing solutions.</p></p>
