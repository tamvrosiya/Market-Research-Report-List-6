<p><p>The suspension arm market is categorized into dependent and independent suspension systems. Dependent suspension, commonly found in heavy-duty vehicles, links wheels on the same axle, offering durability and cost-effectiveness but limited ride comfort. Independent suspension, used in passenger cars, allows each wheel to move independently, enhancing handling, comfort, and stability. While dependent systems are simpler and robust, independent systems provide superior performance and adaptability, catering to modern automotive demands for smoother rides and better control.</p></p>
