<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) systems are designed to improve heart function in patients with heart failure. The market encompasses two primary types: Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-Ds) and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers (CRT-Ps). CRT-Ds combine resynchronization with defibrillation abilities, providing support for patients at risk of life-threatening arrhythmias. In contrast, CRT-Ps focus solely on pacing and synchronization, making them suitable for patients without significant arrhythmia risk, enhancing overall cardiac efficiency and quality of life.</p></p>
