<p><strong>The Ultrasound Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam Sensors</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Retro-Reflective Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound sensors market features various types, including beam sensors, proximity sensors, and ultrasonic retro-reflective sensors. Beam sensors detect the interruption of sound waves between a transmitter and receiver, enabling precise object detection. Proximity sensors utilize ultrasonic waves to measure the distance to an object, ideal for non-contact applications. Ultrasonic retro-reflective sensors combine both methods by reflecting sound waves off surfaces to determine object presence and distance. These technologies enhance automation and safety in diverse industries like manufacturing, automotive, and security.</p></p>
