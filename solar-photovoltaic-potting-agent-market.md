<p><p>The solar photovoltaic potting agent market is segmented into one-component and dual-component potting agents. One-component potting agents are premixed materials that cure through exposure to moisture or heat, offering ease of use and reduced processing time. In contrast, dual-component potting agents require mixing two components before application, providing enhanced mechanical and thermal properties, making them suitable for more demanding environments. Both types are essential for encapsulating solar cells, ensuring durability and protection against environmental factors.</p></p>
