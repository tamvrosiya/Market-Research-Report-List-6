<p><p>The sleeve applicator market comprises two main types: linear and rotary applicators. Linear applicators apply sleeves in a straight line, facilitating high-speed production and precise placement, making them ideal for flat or uniformly shaped products. In contrast, rotary applicators utilize a circular motion, allowing for efficient application on round or cylindrical items. Both types are essential in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods, enhancing packaging efficiency and product presentation.</p></p>
