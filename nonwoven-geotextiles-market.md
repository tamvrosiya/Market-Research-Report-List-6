<p><p>The nonwoven geotextiles market is primarily categorized into types based on the polymer composition: polypropylene (PP), polyethylene (PE), and polyethylene terephthalate (PET). PP geotextiles are known for their durability and UV resistance, making them ideal for erosion control and drainage applications. PET geotextiles offer excellent strength and are often used in reinforcement and filtration projects. PE geotextiles are valued for their chemical resistance and flexibility, suitable for various environmental applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in construction, land development, and environmental management.</p></p>
