Monocrystalline panels, made from single crystal structures, offer higher efficiency and space-saving designs, making them ideal for EV charging where area is limited. In contrast, polycrystalline panels, composed of multiple crystal structures, tend to be more affordable and are suitable for larger installations, though they generally have lower efficiency.
