<p><ul><li>Double layers</li><li>Four layers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Double Surface Anti-Reflective (AR) Glass encompasses various market types based on the number of coating layers. Double-layer AR glass features two thin coatings, optimizing light transmission while minimizing reflections, making it ideal for applications like eyewear and display screens. Four-layer AR glass, on the other hand, incorporates four coatings, further reducing glare and enhancing clarity, suitable for advanced optical devices and high-performance applications. Both types cater to different consumer needs, balancing performance, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
