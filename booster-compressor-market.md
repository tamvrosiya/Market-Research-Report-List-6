<p><p>The booster compressor market includes two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled compressors utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for environments with limited water supply and easier installation. Conversely, water-cooled compressors use water for more efficient heat exchange, resulting in better cooling performance, especially in high-capacity applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs, with air-cooled options often preferred for smaller installations, while water-cooled systems are favored in industrial and larger-scale settings.</p></p>
