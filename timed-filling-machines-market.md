<p><p>Timed filling machines are categorized mainly into automatic and semi-automatic types. Automatic machines operate independently, using precise timers to control the filling process, enhancing efficiency and consistency in high-volume production. They are ideal for large-scale operations. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require some manual input, allowing operators to manage the filling process while still benefiting from timed precision. This type is suitable for smaller operations or varied product lines, offering flexibility with moderate automation.</p></p>
