<p><ul><li>Three-layer Shell</li><li>Durable Water Repellent Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hardshell jacket market features two primary types: three-layer shells and durable water repellent (DWR) coatings. Three-layer shells are constructed from a waterproof membrane sandwiched between a protective outer fabric and a comfort liner, providing superior durability and weather resistance for extreme conditions. In contrast, DWR coatings are applied to the surface of hardshell fabrics, enhancing water resistance while maintaining breathability. Both types cater to outdoor enthusiasts seeking protection against harsh elements, with varying degrees of performance and comfort.</p></p>
