<p><p>The pipetting systems market can be categorized into three main types: automatic, semiautomatic, and manual. Automatic pipetting systems offer high precision and efficiency, utilizing advanced technology for precise liquid handling, making them ideal for high-throughput laboratories. Semiautomatic systems provide a balance between user control and automation, allowing for some manual intervention while still enhancing efficiency. Manual pipetting remains widely used for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for smaller labs or less critical applications where precision is not as critical.</p></p>
