<p><p>The firefighting foam market comprises various types, including protein foam, synthetic foam, and Class A foam. Protein foam is derived from natural proteins and is effective for hydrocarbon fires, forming a robust blanket over the fuel. Synthetic foam, made from synthetic surfactants, is versatile and provides excellent extinguishing properties, particularly for polar solvents. Class A foam, specifically designed for ordinary combustibles like wood and paper, enhances water's effectiveness, reducing surface tension and promoting better penetration during firefighting efforts.</p></p>
