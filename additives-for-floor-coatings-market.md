<p><p>The floor coatings additives market comprises various types that enhance performance and functionality. Anti-foaming additives reduce surface foam during application, ensuring a smooth finish. Wetting and dispersion additives improve the distribution of pigments and fillers, enhancing coat uniformity. Biocidal additives protect against microbial growth. Rheology modification additives adjust viscosity for easier application and improved flow. Impact modification additives enhance the durability and resistance of coatings against mechanical stresses. Other additives may include colorants or UV stabilizers for additional benefits.</p></p>
