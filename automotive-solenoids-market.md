<p><p>The automotive solenoids market comprises various types based on functionality, including 2-way, 3-way, 4-way, and 5-way valves. A 2-way valve controls a single flow path, ideal for basic on/off applications. The 3-way valve directs flow to one of two outlets, commonly used for switching systems. A 4-way valve manages flow in two directions, vital for actuating devices like pneumatic cylinders. The 5-way valve offers an additional port, enhancing complexity in hydraulic or pneumatic systems, improving versatility and control in automotive applications.</p></p>
