<p><p>The portable oxygen meters market includes two primary types: fuel cell oxygen analyzers and zirconia oxygen analyzers. Fuel cell oxygen analyzers utilize electrochemical fuel cell technology, offering high accuracy and rapid response times, making them suitable for various applications such as industrial safety and environmental monitoring. In contrast, zirconia oxygen analyzers operate on the principle of zirconium dioxide, providing reliable measurements at high temperatures, often used in combustion processes and industrial settings. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient oxygen monitoring and control.</p></p>
