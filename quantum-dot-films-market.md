<p><p>Quantum Dot Films are categorized into two main types: Cadmium Quantum Dots and Cadmium-free Quantum Dots. Cadmium Quantum Dots contain cadmium, offering vibrant color representation and high efficiency, making them popular in displays and lighting. However, environmental concerns limit their use. Conversely, Cadmium-free Quantum Dots utilize alternative materials like indium or zinc, providing a more eco-friendly solution without compromising on color quality and performance. This shift aligns with increasing regulatory pressures and consumer preference for sustainable technologies in various applications.</p></p>
