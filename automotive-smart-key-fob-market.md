<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive smart key fob market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES enables users to unlock and start their vehicles without physically pressing buttons, utilizing proximity sensors for convenience. Conversely, RKES requires the user to press buttons on the fob to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance vehicle security and owner convenience, appealing to modern consumers seeking advanced automotive technology.</p></p>
