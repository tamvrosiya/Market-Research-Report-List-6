<p><p>The artificial teeth market comprises three main types: all-ceramic, metal-ceramic, and all-metal. All-ceramic teeth are favored for their aesthetic appeal and natural appearance, making them ideal for front teeth restoration. Metal-ceramic teeth combine the strength of metal with the natural look of porcelain, offering durability for various dental applications. All-metal teeth, often made from gold or other alloys, are highly durable and suitable for posterior teeth, where strength is paramount. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional dental needs.</p></p>
