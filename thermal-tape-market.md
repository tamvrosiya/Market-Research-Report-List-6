<p><p>The thermal tape market is segmented into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided thermal tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for applications requiring heat transfer from a component to a heat sink or other surfaces. In contrast, double-sided thermal tape has adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced bonding for components that need to be affixed to surfaces while also facilitating heat transfer. These tapes are commonly used in electronics, automotive, and other industries needing efficient thermal management solutions.</p></p>
