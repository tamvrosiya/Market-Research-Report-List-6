<p><strong>The Dry Bulk Shipping Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time Charter</li><li>Voyage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry bulk shipping service market primarily comprises two types: time charter and voyage charter. In a time charter, the shipowner leases the vessel for a specified period, granting the charterer control over its operations while covering operational costs. Conversely, in a voyage charter, the shipowner agrees to transport cargo for a specific journey, with the charterer paying a fee based on the voyage's distance and cargo quantity, leaving operational control with the shipowner. Each type offers flexibility tailored to varying shipping needs.</p></p>
