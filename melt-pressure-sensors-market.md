<p><p>Melt pressure sensors are essential in monitoring and controlling the pressure of molten materials in processing applications. The market can be segmented into two main types: NaK filled melt pressure sensors and oil filled melt pressure sensors. NaK filled sensors utilize a sodium-potassium alloy for superior thermal conductivity and response times, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Conversely, oil filled sensors use a hydraulic oil medium, providing reliability and stability in various operating conditions, suitable for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
