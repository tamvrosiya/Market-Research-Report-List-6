<p><p>The Polyethersulfone (PES) membrane filters market is categorized based on pore size, specifically into two segments: pore size ≤ 0.5µm and pore size > 0.5µm. Filters with pore size ≤ 0.5µm are primarily used for ultrafiltration applications, effectively removing bacteria and viruses, making them crucial in pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical processes. On the other hand, filters with pore size > 0.5µm typically cater to microfiltration applications, aimed at removing larger particles, turbidity, and sediments in various industries, including water treatment and food processing.</p></p>
