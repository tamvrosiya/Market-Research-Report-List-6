<p><p>The single-phase transformer market is categorized into low frequency, intermediate frequency, and high frequency segments. Low-frequency transformers typically operate at 50/60 Hz, commonly used in power distribution. Intermediate frequency transformers, ranging from 400 Hz to several kHz, are often found in aviation and military applications. High-frequency transformers, operating above 10 kHz, are utilized in telecommunications and high-efficiency power supplies, featuring smaller, lightweight designs. Each segment serves specific applications, driving innovation and tailored solutions in the transformer industry.</p></p>
