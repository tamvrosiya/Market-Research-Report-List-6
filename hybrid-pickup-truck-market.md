<p><p>The hybrid pickup truck market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Plug-in Hybrid Electric Pickup Trucks**: These vehicles offer a larger battery that can be charged through a power outlet, allowing for extended electric-only driving ranges combined with a gasoline engine for flexibility.</p><p>2. **Mild Hybrid Electric Pickup Trucks**: Utilizing a smaller battery, these trucks provide energy assistance to the gasoline engine, improving efficiency without enabling full electric-only operation.</p><p>3. **Full Hybrid Electric Pickup Trucks**: Capable of operating on electric power alone, these trucks combine a gasoline engine and electric motor for optimal fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.</p></p>
