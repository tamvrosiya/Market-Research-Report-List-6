<p><p>The aerospace high-performance alloys market is categorized into three main types: iron-based, cobalt-based, and nickel-based alloys. Iron-based alloys, known for their strength and cost-effectiveness, are used in applications such as landing gear. Cobalt-based alloys offer exceptional wear resistance and strength at high temperatures, making them suitable for components in gas turbines. Nickel-based alloys are favored for their excellent corrosion resistance and thermal stability, primarily utilized in aerospace engines and other critical high-temperature components, ensuring safety and performance.</p></p>
