<p><strong>The Diesel Common Rail Direct Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solenoid Type</li><li>Piezo Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI) System market is primarily divided into two types: Solenoid Type and Piezo Type injectors. Solenoid Type injectors use electromagnetic solenoids to control fuel injection timing and quantity, which allows for efficient fuel delivery and reduced emissions. On the other hand, Piezo Type injectors utilize piezoelectric materials to achieve faster and more precise control of injection, leading to improved combustion efficiency and performance. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing diesel engine efficiency and meeting emissions standards.</p></p>
