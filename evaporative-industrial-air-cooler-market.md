<p><p>Evaporative industrial air coolers are energy-efficient cooling systems that use the natural process of evaporation to lower temperatures in industrial settings. These coolers work by drawing warm air through water-saturated pads, where the air is cooled and then circulated throughout the space. Their growing popularity is attributed to their low energy consumption, eco-friendliness, and effectiveness in dry climates.</p><p>The Evaporative Industrial Air Cooler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
