<p><p>The reboiler market consists of various types tailored for specific applications. Kettle type reboilers are widely used for their simplicity and efficiency in boiling liquids. Thermosyphon reboilers utilize natural circulation to enhance heat transfer, making them energy-efficient. Fired reboilers rely on external combustion for heating, suitable for high-temperature requirements. Forced circulation reboilers use pumps to improve fluid flow, ensuring consistent performance in large industrial processes. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse process needs in industries like petrochemicals and refining.</p></p>
