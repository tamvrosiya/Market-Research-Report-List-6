<p><ul><li>Contact Probe</li><li>Non-Contact Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine tool probe market is segmented into two primary types: contact probes and non-contact probes. Contact probes physically touch the workpiece to gather dimensional and surface information, providing high accuracy and reliability for various machining operations. In contrast, non-contact probes utilize technologies like laser or vision systems to measure surfaces without physical interaction, allowing for faster inspection and reduced risk of damage. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing manufacturing precision and efficiency in modern machining processes.</p></p>
