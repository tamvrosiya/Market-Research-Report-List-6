<p><ul><li>Gear pump</li><li>Screw pump</li><li>Vane pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric-driven drum pump market features three main types: gear pumps, screw pumps, and vane pumps. Gear pumps utilize meshing gears to transfer fluid, ensuring high pressure and efficiency. Screw pumps operate by drawing fluid through a helical screw mechanism, providing a steady flow ideal for viscous liquids. Vane pumps use rotating vanes within a chamber to create suction and discharge, offering versatility in handling various fluids. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing fluid transfer reliability and effectiveness.</p></p>
