<p><p>The **Automotive Neutral Safety Switches** market includes various types designed to ensure safe vehicle operation. **Push On Automotive Neutral Safety Switches** activate when pressed, enabling the ignition only in neutral or park. **Push Off Automotive Neutral Safety Switches** deactivate when pressed, preventing ignition in gear. **Normally Opened Automotive Neutral Safety Switches** remain open until engaged, allowing current flow when in neutral. **Normally Closed Automotive Neutral Safety Switches** stay closed until disengaged, interrupting current flow when not in neutral. Each type enhances safety by preventing unintended vehicle movement.</p></p>
