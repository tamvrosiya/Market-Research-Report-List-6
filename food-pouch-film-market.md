<p><p>The food pouch film market encompasses various types of films used for packaging. Aluminum foil provides excellent barrier properties against moisture and light. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film is lightweight and offers good clarity and printability. BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) film is known for its high tensile strength and oxygen barrier. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) film is durable and resistant to chemicals. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film is flexible and heat sealable, while LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) film offers toughness and elasticity. Other materials may include specialized blends and coatings tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
