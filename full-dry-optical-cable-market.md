<p><strong>The Full Dry Optical Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton</li><li>Stranded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Dry Optical Cable market consists of two primary types: skeleton and stranded cables. Skeleton cables are characterized by a centralized strength member with loose optical fibers arranged around it, providing flexibility and ease of installation. In contrast, stranded cables feature optical fibers stranded around a central core, enhancing durability and reducing weight. Both designs aim to optimize performance in various environmental conditions, catering to different applications in telecommunications and data transmission networks while ensuring reliable connectivity.</p></p>
