<p><p>The melanoma molecular diagnostics market encompasses several key technologies. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling detection of mutations associated with melanoma. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) visualizes genetic abnormalities in tumor cells using fluorescent probes. DNA Sequencing provides detailed information about genetic variations, assisting in treatment decisions. Gene Chips analyze multiple genes simultaneously, allowing for high-throughput screening of gene expression and mutations. Together, these technologies enhance the diagnosis and management of melanoma by providing critical molecular insights.</p></p>
