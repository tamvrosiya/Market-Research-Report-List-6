<p><strong>The Anesthetic Vaporizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plenum Vaporizers</li><li>Drawover Vaporizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anesthetic vaporizer market comprises primarily two types: plenum vaporizers and draw-over vaporizers. Plenum vaporizers are designed for use in modern anesthesia machines, where they deliver precise concentrations of anesthetic agents by blending them with fresh gas flow, ensuring stability and consistency. In contrast, draw-over vaporizers function by using the patient's breathing to draw gas through the vaporizing chamber, making them suitable for low-resource settings or portable applications. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and environments.</p></p>
