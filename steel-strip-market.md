<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled</li><li>Cold Rolled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel strip market includes **Hot Rolled** and **Cold Rolled** types. **Hot Rolled** steel strips are produced by heating steel above its recrystallization temperature, making them easier to shape and more cost-effective. They have a rough surface and are used in construction, automotive frames, and machinery. **Cold Rolled** steel strips are processed further at room temperature, resulting in a smoother finish, tighter tolerances, and higher strength. They are ideal for precision applications like appliances, automotive panels, and electronics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
