<p><p>The angle displacement sensor market comprises various types, including optical, capacitive, inductive, and Hall effect sensors. Optical sensors use light to detect position changes, offering high precision and resolution. Capacitive sensors measure angle through changes in capacitance, beneficial for contactless applications. Inductive sensors operate based on changes in inductance related to angular movement, often used in harsh environments. Hall effect sensors detect magnetic fields to determine angular position, providing reliability and robustness, particularly in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
