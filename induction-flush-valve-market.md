<p><p>The induction flush valve market comprises two main types: hidden induction flush valves and surface-mounted induction flush valves. Hidden induction flush valves are installed within the wall, providing a sleek and aesthetic appearance while ensuring hygiene by minimizing touchpoints. In contrast, surface-mounted induction flush valves are attached to the wall, offering easier installation and maintenance. Both types use sensor technology to activate flushing automatically, promoting water conservation and enhanced sanitation in public restrooms and commercial facilities.</p></p>
