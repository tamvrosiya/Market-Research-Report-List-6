<p><strong>The Indoor Smart Bikes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resistance</li><li>Felt Pad Resistance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indoor smart bikes primarily utilize two types of resistance systems: magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance. Magnetic resistance offers a smooth and silent workout by using magnets to create friction without physical contact, allowing for easier adjustment and reduced wear. In contrast, felt pad resistance relies on a pad that presses against the flywheel, generating resistance through friction. While typically more affordable, felt pad systems can be noisier and require more maintenance. Both systems cater to different user preferences in the smart bike market.</p></p>
