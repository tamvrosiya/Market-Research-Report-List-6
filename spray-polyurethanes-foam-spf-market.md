<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material used in construction and industrial applications. The market is primarily segmented into two types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell SPF is lightweight, flexible, and offers better sound absorption, making it ideal for interior insulation. In contrast, closed cell SPF is denser, more rigid, and provides superior moisture resistance, suitable for exterior applications. Other variations may include specialty foams designed for specific uses, enhancing thermal performance and structural integrity.</p></p>
