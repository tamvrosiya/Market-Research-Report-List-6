<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) equipment market encompasses technologies like Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm. Polygon Mirror LDI utilizes a rotating mirror to focus laser beams, enabling high-resolution imaging at 365nm wavelengths, ideal for intricate patterns in printed circuit board (PCB) manufacturing. In contrast, DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) at 405nm employs micro-mirrors to produce precise imagery, offering versatility for various applications, including photomasks and advanced packaging. Both technologies drive efficiency and accuracy in photolithography processes.</p></p>
