<p><ul><li>Coated With Thin Layer Of Aluminium / Silicon Alloy</li><li>Coated With Pure Aluminum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminized steel market features two main types of coatings: those coated with a thin layer of aluminum/silicon alloy and those coated with pure aluminum. The aluminum/silicon alloy offers enhanced corrosion resistance and improved ductility, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, pure aluminum coatings provide excellent barrier properties against moisture and rust, making them ideal for environments with high humidity. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, construction, and electrical, due to their durability and performance characteristics.</p></p>
