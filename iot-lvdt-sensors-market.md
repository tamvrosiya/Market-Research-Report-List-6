<p><p>The IoT LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) sensors market is divided into two primary types: DC LVDT sensors and AC LVDT sensors. DC LVDT sensors use direct current to provide precise linear measurements and are known for their stability and low noise, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, AC LVDT sensors operate on alternating current, offering advantages in dynamic measurements over longer distances. Both types are integral for real-time monitoring in IoT applications across diverse sectors.</p></p>
