<p><p>Frequency Select MEMS Oscillators (FSMOs) are precision timing devices that utilize microelectromechanical systems technology to provide stable frequency outputs. The market for FSMOs is categorized into two main types: Surface-Mount Device (SMD) packages and Chip-Scale Packages (CSP). SMD packages are designed for easy soldering onto printed circuit boards, offering robust performance in various applications. In contrast, CSPs are miniaturized versions that integrate the oscillator directly into the circuitry, enabling higher density and reduced size in electronic devices.</p></p>
