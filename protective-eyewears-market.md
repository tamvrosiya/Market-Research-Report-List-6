<p><p>The protective eyewear market comprises three main types: Direct Vented, Indirect Vented, and Non-vented protective eyewear. Direct Vented eyewear features holes that allow air circulation while preventing large particles from entering, making them suitable for environments requiring airflow. Indirect Vented eyewear utilizes channels to provide ventilation without direct exposure, ideal for hazardous settings. Non-vented eyewear offers a sealed design to block dust and chemicals completely, providing maximum protection in high-risk areas where airflow is not a concern.</p></p>
