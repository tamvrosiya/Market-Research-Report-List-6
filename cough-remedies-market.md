<p><p>The cough remedies market is segmented into several key types, each addressing specific symptoms. Antihistamines alleviate allergy-related coughs by blocking histamine, while expectorants help loosen mucus, making it easier to expel. Bronchodilators relax bronchial muscles, easing airflow for those with asthma or COPD. Decongestants reduce nasal swelling, relieving cough caused by congestion. Antibiotics target bacterial infections when a cough is due to underlying respiratory conditions. Together, these categories offer diverse treatment options for various cough-related issues.</p></p>
