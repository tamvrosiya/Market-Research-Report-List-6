<p><p>The outdoor backpacks and rucksacks market features three main types: frameless backpacks, internal-frame backpacks, and external-frame backpacks. Frameless backpacks are lightweight and ideal for minimalist trips, offering flexibility but limited support. Internal-frame backpacks distribute weight efficiently, providing good support and stability, making them popular for trekking. External-frame backpacks have a rigid frame that allows for ventilation and easy load management, suitable for carrying heavy loads. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in outdoor adventures.</p></p>
