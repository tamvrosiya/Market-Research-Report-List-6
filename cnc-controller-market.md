<p><p>The CNC controller market consists of two primary types: CNC machines and CNC controllers. CNC machines are automated tools that perform precise cutting, milling, and fabrication tasks in various materials, leveraging advanced technology for efficient production. CNC controllers, on the other hand, are the software and hardware systems that manage the operation of CNC machines, translating design specifications into actionable commands. Together, they enhance productivity and precision across industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
