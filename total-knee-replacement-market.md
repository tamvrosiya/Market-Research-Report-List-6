<p><p>The Total Knee Replacement market primarily consists of two types: Primary Knee Systems and Partial Knee Systems. Primary Knee Systems are designed for patients with extensive knee joint damage, providing a complete replacement of the joint. In contrast, Partial Knee Systems are used for individuals with localized knee issues, replacing only the damaged portion while preserving healthy tissue. Both types cater to specific patient needs and conditions, enhancing mobility and alleviating pain through advanced surgical technologies.</p></p>
