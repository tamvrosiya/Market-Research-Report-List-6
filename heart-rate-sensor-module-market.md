<p><ul><li>Photoplethysmography Sensors Module</li><li>Electrocardiography Sensors Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart rate sensor modules are classified into two main types: Photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors and Electrocardiography (ECG) sensors. PPG sensors measure blood volume changes in microvascular tissues through light absorption, making them suitable for wearable devices. In contrast, ECG sensors detect the electrical activity of the heart, providing detailed insights into heart rhythms and conditions. Both technologies play crucial roles in health monitoring and fitness applications, catering to a growing demand for real-time cardiovascular data among consumers and healthcare professionals.</p></p>
