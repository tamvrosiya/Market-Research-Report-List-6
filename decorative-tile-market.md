<p><p>The decorative tile market comprises three main types: ceramic tile, porcelain tile, and stone tile. Ceramic tiles are versatile and affordable, often featuring colorful glazes, making them suitable for various applications. Porcelain tiles are denser and more durable, ideal for high-moisture areas due to their low water absorption. Stone tiles, including marble and granite, offer a natural aesthetic with unique textures and patterns but require more maintenance. Each type caters to different design needs and functional requirements in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
