<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Structural Steel Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW)</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld (ERW)</li><li>Seamless (SMLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural steel pipe market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Spiral Weld Pipe, fabricated by spirally welding steel strips, offers high strength and is ideal for large diameters. Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW) pipes are known for their durability and are used in high-pressure applications. Electric Resistance Weld (ERW) pipes are produced using electric resistance heating, suitable for lighter applications. Seamless (SMLS) pipes, manufactured without seams, provide superior strength and are preferred in high-stress environments.</p></p>
