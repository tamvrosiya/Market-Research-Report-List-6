<p><p>The pneumatic quarter-turn actuator market consists of several types, primarily including pneumatic single-acting and double-acting quarter-turn actuators, along with other variations. </p><p>Pneumatic single-acting quarter-turn actuators utilize air pressure to move in one direction, requiring a spring or gravity for the return motion. In contrast, pneumatic double-acting quarter-turn actuators use air pressure for both the opening and closing motions, providing greater control and efficiency. Other types may include specialized designs for unique applications, enhancing versatility in various industrial settings.</p></p>
