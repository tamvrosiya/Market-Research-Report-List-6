<p><p>The Natural Rubber Sheets market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I sheets are typically produced using the coagulation method, resulting in high-quality, uniform sheets suitable for various applications, including tires and footwear. Type II sheets, on the other hand, are manufactured through the smoke-drying process and are generally more affordable but may have lower quality and durability. These distinctions influence their uses across industries, with Type I favored for premium applications and Type II for cost-sensitive markets.</p></p>
