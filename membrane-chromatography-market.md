<p><strong>The Membrane Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography</li><li>Affinity Film Chromatography</li><li>Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane chromatography encompasses various types, each serving distinct separation needs. Ion exchange membrane chromatography utilizes charged membranes for the selective binding of ionic substances, facilitating purification in bioprocessing. Affinity film chromatography leverages specific interactions, targeting biomolecules like proteins or antibodies, enhancing selectivity. Hydrophobic interaction membrane chromatography relies on hydrophobic interactions to separate proteins based on their hydrophobicity, enabling effective purification. Together, these techniques offer versatile and efficient solutions for downstream processing in biotechnology and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
