<p><ul><li>Single-Acting Rephasing Cylinders</li><li>Double-Acting Rephasing Cylinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rephasing hydraulic cylinders market comprises two main types: single-acting and double-acting rephasing cylinders. Single-acting rephasing cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure for movement in one direction, relying on spring action or gravitational force for retraction, making them simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, double-acting rephasing cylinders use hydraulic pressure for both extension and retraction, allowing for more precise control and power in various applications. Both types are crucial in industries like construction, manufacturing, and agriculture for synchronized motion.</p></p>
