<p><ul><li>Wire-wound Silicon Rubber Heater</li><li>Etched-foil Silicon Rubber Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon rubber heating elements are versatile heating solutions widely used in various applications. The wire-wound silicon rubber heater features a wire resistance element embedded within a silicone rubber layer, providing uniform heat distribution and flexibility. In contrast, etched-foil silicon rubber heaters utilize thin metal foils that are precisely etched to create heating patterns, allowing for efficient heat transfer and customization. Both types offer durability, moisture resistance, and adaptability, making them suitable for industrial, medical, and consumer applications.</p></p>
