<p><p>The area luminaires market is divided into two primary types: recessed and surface-mounted. Recessed luminaires are installed above the ceiling line, providing a sleek look while offering uniform lighting that minimizes glare. They are ideal for commercial and residential spaces where aesthetics matter. Surface-mounted luminaires, on the other hand, are affixed directly to ceilings or walls, making them versatile for various environments, including outdoor settings. This type typically features a more robust design, catering to higher durability demands.</p></p>
