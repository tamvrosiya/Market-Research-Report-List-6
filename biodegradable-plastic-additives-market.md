<p><p>The biodegradable plastic additives market encompasses two main types: oxo-biodegradable additives and additives with organic materials. Oxo-biodegradable additives promote plastic degradation through oxidation, enabling plastics to break down more quickly in the environment. In contrast, additives with organic materials incorporate natural components, facilitating microbial degradation and enhancing the compostability of plastics. Both types aim to reduce plastic waste and environmental impact, making plastics more sustainable while responding to growing ecological concerns and regulatory pressures for greener materials.</p></p>
