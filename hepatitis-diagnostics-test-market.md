<p><p>The hepatitis diagnostics test market includes three primary types: blood tests, imaging tests, and liver biopsies. Blood tests detect specific viral antigens, antibodies, or liver enzymes, providing primary screening for hepatitis infections. Imaging tests, such as ultrasound and elastography, assess liver condition and damage without invasive procedures. Liver biopsies involve extracting liver tissue to evaluate the severity of liver damage and inflammation. Together, these methods help diagnose hepatitis, guide treatment decisions, and monitor disease progression.</p></p>
