<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle market comprises three main types: Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), and Battery Electric Vehicles (EVs). HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing partial electric operation without plugging in. PHEVs offer both electric and gasoline power, enabling charging through an external source for longer electric drives. EVs run solely on electricity, requiring charging stations. This market aims to reduce emissions and promote sustainable transportation.</p></p>
