One-component gels are pre-mixed and ready for immediate application, offering convenience and consistent performance for various electronic devices. In contrast, two-component gels require a mixing process before application, allowing for customizable viscosity and curing properties. Both types are used in electronics cooling applications, with one-component gels favored for ease of use and two-component gels preferred for specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
