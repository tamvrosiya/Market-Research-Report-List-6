<p><p>The nanoclay market primarily comprises two types: Kaolinite and Smectite. Kaolinite is known for its high purity and is widely used in ceramics, paper, and cosmetics due to its fine particle size and chemical stability. Smectite, characterized by its swelling properties, is prominent in applications such as drilling muds and sealants. Both types enhance mechanical properties and durability in various formulations, making them essential in industries like construction, automotive, and electronics, where advanced materials are increasingly demanded.</p></p>
