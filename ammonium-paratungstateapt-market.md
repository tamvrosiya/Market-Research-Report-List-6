<p><p>Ammonium Paratungstate (APT) is a key intermediate in tungsten production, primarily used to manufacture tungsten metal and compounds. The APT market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Neutralization** – A process involving the reaction of ammonium hydroxide with tungsten solutions to produce APT.</p><p>2. **Tungsten Acid Method** – This involves dissolving tungsten ores in acid, followed by ammonium addition to precipitate APT.</p><p>3. **Thermal Decomposition Method** – This method involves heating tungsten compounds to produce APT through thermal processes. Each method influences the purity, cost, and efficiency of APT production.</p></p>
