<p><p>Feed mycotoxin binders and modifiers are essential in the livestock industry to mitigate the harmful effects of mycotoxins in animal feed. Binders are substances that chemically bind to mycotoxins, preventing their absorption in the gastrointestinal tract, thus reducing toxicity. Modifiers, on the other hand, alter the structure of mycotoxins, making them less harmful or more easily excretable. Both types contribute to improved animal health, performance, and feed efficiency while minimizing the risks associated with contaminated feed.</p></p>
