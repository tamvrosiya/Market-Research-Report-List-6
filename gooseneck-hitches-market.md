<p><p>Gooseneck hitches are towing systems designed for heavy-duty hauling, commonly used in trucks. The market is segmented into above-bed and under-bed installation types. Above-bed installation involves mounting the hitch ball over the truck bed, providing a direct connection for trailers, often requiring a hole in the bed. Under-bed installation places the hitch beneath the truck bed, offering a cleaner look and preserving bed space but requiring more complex installation. Both types cater to different user preferences for functionality and aesthetics.</p></p>
