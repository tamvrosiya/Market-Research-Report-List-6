<p><p>Robotic Milking Systems (RMS) automate the milking process, enhancing efficiency and animal welfare. In the market, there are three main types: Standalone Units, designed for individual cows and typically suited for smaller herds; Multiple Stall Units, which accommodate several cows simultaneously, suitable for medium-sized farms; and Rotary Units, where cows rotate through a milking platform, ideal for large-scale operations. Each type offers distinct benefits, such as labor savings, increased milk yield, and improved herd management.</p></p>
