<p><p>The inguinal hernia graft market is categorized into two primary types: direct inguinal hernia grafts and indirect inguinal hernia grafts. Direct inguinal hernias occur when tissue protrudes through a weak spot in the abdominal muscles, typically affecting middle-aged men. Conversely, indirect inguinal hernias result from an opening in the inguinal canal, often seen in younger individuals, including infants. Each type requires specific surgical techniques and graft materials, influencing the market's growth, demand, and technological advancements.</p></p>
