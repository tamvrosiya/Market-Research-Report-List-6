<p><p>The Infrared Band Fiber Optical Spectrometer market comprises two main types: Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers and Multimode Fiber Spectrometers. Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers utilize a single light path, allowing for higher resolution and sensitivity, making them ideal for precise measurements in applications like telecommunications and sensing. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Spectrometers support multiple light paths, enabling broader bandwidth and faster data transmission, which is suitable for applications needing higher light intensity, such as industrial and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
