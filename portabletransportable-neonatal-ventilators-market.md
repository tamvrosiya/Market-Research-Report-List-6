<p><p>The portable/transportable neonatal ventilators market is categorized into invasive and non-invasive ventilators. Invasive ventilators involve the insertion of a tube into the airway, offering precise control of breathing functions for critically ill newborns. These are typically used in intensive care settings. Non-invasive ventilators, on the other hand, provide respiratory support through masks, reducing discomfort and risk of infection while being suitable for less severe cases. Both types facilitate better management of neonatal respiratory distress, enhancing treatment options for vulnerable infants.</p></p>
