<p><p>The COVID-19 vaccine market encompasses various types based on their technology. DNA vaccines use engineered plasmids to induce an immune response. Non-replicating viral vector vaccines employ harmless viruses to deliver genetic material coding for the SARS-CoV-2 spike protein. Protein subunit vaccines contain harmless pieces of the virus to trigger immunity. RNA vaccines utilize messenger RNA to instruct cells to produce the viral protein. Other types include live attenuated and inactivated virus vaccines, offering diverse approaches to immunity against COVID-19.</p></p>
