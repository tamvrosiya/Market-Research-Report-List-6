<p><p>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) resin market is primarily segmented into two types: Tubular EVA and Autoclave EVA. Tubular EVA is produced through a continuous process that allows for high production rates and consistent quality, making it ideal for applications such as packaging and footwear. In contrast, Autoclave EVA is manufactured in batch processes under high pressure and temperature, resulting in a higher degree of polymerization. This type is typically used in specialized applications like adhesives and coatings due to its superior properties.</p></p>
