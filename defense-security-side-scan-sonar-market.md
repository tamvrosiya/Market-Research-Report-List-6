<p><p>The Defense & Security Side Scan Sonar market comprises two main types: single-beam and multi-beam systems. Single-beam sonar focuses sonar pulses in one direction, providing detailed images of the seafloor but covering a limited area, making it suitable for targeted reconnaissance. In contrast, multi-beam sonar emits multiple beams simultaneously, enabling broader area coverage and faster data collection, which is ideal for comprehensive surveillance and mapping of underwater environments. Both types are crucial for enhancing maritime security and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
