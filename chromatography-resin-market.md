<p><p>The chromatography resin market comprises various types tailored for specific separation techniques. Size exclusion chromatography resin separates molecules based on size, while ion exchange chromatography resin targets molecules by charge. Hydrophobic interaction chromatography resin utilizes hydrophobicity to separate proteins, and mixed-mode chromatography resin combines different separation mechanisms for enhanced performance. Affinity chromatography resin selectively captures target biomolecules using specific ligands, with protein A chromatography resin specializing in antibody purification through its affinity for the Fc region of antibodies.</p></p>
