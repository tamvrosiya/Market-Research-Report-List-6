<p><p>The Camera Module Insulating Adhesives market comprises two primary types: UV Curable Type and Non-Curable Type. UV Curable Adhesives are reactive formulations that solidify upon exposure to ultraviolet light, offering rapid curing and excellent adhesion for camera modules, essential for high-performance applications. Conversely, Non-Curable Adhesives do not require any curing process and can be used for applications needing flexibility and ease of use. Both types are critical for enhancing the durability and functionality of camera modules in various electronic devices.</p></p>
