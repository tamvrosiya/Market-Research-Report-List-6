<p><ul><li>Hard Tissue</li><li>Soft Tissue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The regenerative dentistry market is divided into two primary types: hard tissue and soft tissue. Hard tissue regeneration focuses on repairing or replacing tooth structures such as enamel, dentin, and bone, often utilizing materials like dental implants and grafts. In contrast, soft tissue regeneration aims to restore or enhance gingival (gum) tissue health, employing methods like guided tissue regeneration and various biomaterials to promote healing and growth. Together, these approaches enhance dental health and aesthetics, addressing diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
