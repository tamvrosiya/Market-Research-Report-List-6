<p><p>The veterinary X-ray system market is primarily divided into two types: digital radiography and analog radiography. Digital radiography utilizes digital sensors to capture high-quality images instantly, allowing for enhanced image processing and storage, leading to faster diagnosis and analysis. In contrast, analog radiography relies on traditional film-based methods, which require chemical processing and are less efficient in terms of image clarity and speed. Both systems serve essential roles in veterinary practices, but digital radiography is increasingly preferred for its advanced capabilities.</p></p>
